23rd WORLD MUSIC FESTIVAL CHICAGO 2023

In its 23rd year of programming, the festival will showcase captivating performances in 11 distinct indoor and outdoor venues throughout Chicago. Attendees will have the opportunity to immerse themselves in a mesmerizing array of world music and its various subgenres, hailing from culturally rich regions like Brazil, South Korea, and Senegal.

Please contact us per Email for any further questions about World Music Festival Chicago 2023!
